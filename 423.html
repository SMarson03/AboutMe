ABOUT ME

    My parents and grandparents encouraged me to be an creative. I believe my first painting was of a river and some tree at eight years old. I gifted it to my grandmother and she kept it for years.

    Every week I see at least one new art show. Especially when I feel overwhelmed, I need a visual refresher. One of my favorite new artists is Danielle Mckinney.
